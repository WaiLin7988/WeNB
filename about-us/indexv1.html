Our Journey

From homesick students to a shared table of comfort. This is the story of WeNB — carrying the scent of home and the warmth of Burmese culture in every bowl.

  • It Began with Homesickness

    A small craving for something familiar sparked the idea of steaming bananas with milk after class.

  • Shared Among Friends

    One small idea grew brighter with every story we shared. What began as casual talks with friends soon became a recipe for comfort.

  • A Name Was Born

    “We Need Banana” — playful and honest, capturing both our craving and our comfort.

  • Today

    WeNB carries the scent of home to Burmese students in Bangkok and invites Thai and international friends to discover our culture.

We Need Banana — Burmese steamed milk banana for students in Bangkok. Comfort. Culture. Community.

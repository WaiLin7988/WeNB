Our Journey

From homesick students to a shared table of comfort. This is the story of WeNB — carrying the scent of home and the warmth of Burmese culture in every bowl.

  • It Began with Homesickness

    A small craving for something familiar sparked the idea of steaming bananas with milk after class, a recipe passed down from our grandmothers.

  • Shared Among Friends

    One small idea grew brighter with every story we shared. What began as casual talks with friends soon became a recipe for comfort and community.

  • A Name Was Born

    “We Need Banana” — playful and honest, capturing both our craving and our comfort. It was the only name that felt right.

  • Today

    WeNB carries the scent of home to Burmese students in Bangkok and invites Thai and international friends to discover our culture, one warm dessert at a time.

A Note from Our Founders

As a student group from Myanmar studying in Bangkok, we know what it's like to miss home. We started WeNB because we wanted a way to connect with our roots and share a piece of our culture. It's not just a dessert; it's a piece of our hearts, baked into every warm bowl. Thank you for being a part of our story.
